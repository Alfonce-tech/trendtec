Product Features:
 
Powerful Rotary Motor & Strong Shear Force

The powerful motor supports professional removal, catching and shaving more hair at once and saves you more time.
Its maximum rotary speed (Turbo Mode) reaches 6000 RPM, delivering maximum power when working with coarse and dense hair.

User-Friendly
Quick and Easy

Designed with professional-grade at-home haircuts in mind, SmartClipper Plus uses ultra-sharp and wear-resistant blades that can cut through coarse and dense hair.
The 4 guide combs included can better help novices quickly cut hair according to the length they want, preventing stabbing the scalp and ensuring that the haircut is neat.
Type-C Charging Port
Universal Charging & Long-lasting Battery

This salon-quality smart clipper contains a long-lasting lithium-ion battery that lasts for at least two hours on a full charge.
The Type-C charging port supports both double-sided insertion of the USB interface and fast charging.
Adjustable Blades
Meeting Diverse Hair-cutting Needs

You can easily control the length of the haircut by turning the knob at the bottom as well as using the 4 guide combs in different sizes to achieve a precise cut according to your needs.

Comfort Ergo Grip

Simple and elegant, oraimo SmartClipper Plus adopts a special cylindrical design. Its ergonomic grip area enables different and comfortable grip positions.
