Product Features:

 

2.01" HD Screen

Better User Experience

The large screen not only makes the operation smoother but also enhances visual experience with more vivid colors and clearer displays.

 

Tempered Glass

Anti-scratch And Explosion-proof

Watch Nova V's display screen is highly adaptable and durable, as it is made of tempered glass that offers strong resistance to scratches and impacts.

 

Dynamic Video Watch Faces

Diverse And Immersive Experiences

Watch Nova V displays videos on the dial, weather and sports pages. The videos change according to the type of exercise and the weather conditions, giving you an immersive experience.

 

Camera Remote, Preview Sync

Meets The Needs of Long-distance Photography

Watch Nova V can sync photography page with mobile phone through the oraimo health app, allowing for camera control and instant preview directly on your wrist. The photography is no longer limited by distance.

 

1ATM Waterproof

Upgraded Waterproof Smart Watch

With 1ATM rating waterproof, Watch Nova V can withstand pressures equivalent to a depth of 10 meters*. Have no fear of water splash or sudden rain, as your watch is well-protected for splash, water, and dust resistance.

*Under ideal conditions. In practical applications, 1ATM indicates that the product has a certain level of water resistance.

 

100+ Sport Modes

Satisfies Various Exercise Needs

Watch Nova V, featuring more than 100 sports modes such as outdoor running, treadmill, and cycling, meets diverse exercise needs. It accurately tracks your performance during exercise and enables the one-click sharing of exercise data and scenery encountered in sports to social media.

 

Wireless Call

Stay Connected On the Go

Make calls directly on your wrist, without the hassle of picking up your phone. 

 

24-hour Health Monitoring

Prompt Feedback on Health

Watch Nova V offers 24-hour health monitoring, including functions such as Daily Activities, Heart Rate, Blood Oxygen, Breathing Exercises, and Sleep, providing timely data feedback. For convenient access to data from the monitoring, download the oraimo health app.

 

Integrated Music Player

Listen Anytime, Anywhere You Want

Watch Nova V supports local music and music played through the oraimo health app on your phone. You can control the music pause and switch tracks on the watch directly.

 

Up to 5-day Usage Time

Long Using Time

You can charge the watch while showering or tooth-brushing in the morning; its lithium polymer battery lasts up to 5 days after a full charge. No need for charging on travels or business trips.

*Using Time: 5 days for typical use, 3 days for heavy use
