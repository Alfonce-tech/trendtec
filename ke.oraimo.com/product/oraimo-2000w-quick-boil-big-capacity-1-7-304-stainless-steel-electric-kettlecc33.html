Product Features:

2000W Quick Boil

Drink Boiled Water Quickly

The SmartKettle Byte is capable of boiling at 2000 watts, which can boil water in a few seconds, thereby shortening your waiting time.

Food-grade 304 Stainless Steel

Drink Safe, Odor-free Hot Wate

The interior of SmartKettle Byte is made of food-grade 304 stainless steel, which is BPA-Free, Odor-free, Heat-resistant and Rust-resistant. So the water is safer and it is easier to clean.

Intelligent Heat Preservation

Meet Multiple Water Drinking Needs

You can choose insulation at 90 , 80 , 60 , 50 , and 40 , corresponding to different water needs, such as 90 C for boiled instant noodles and 40 C for direct drinking.

Visible Water Temperature

Drink Water at a Suitable Temperature

Just one glance and you can understand the water temperature. No worry about being scalded by hot water or the water being too cold to be used for brewing.

One-touch Boil Button

Convenient And Fast Boiling of Water

Just press the boil button, no need to adjust anything else, and SmartKettle Byte will start boiling water.

1.7L Big Capacity

Meet The Whole Day's Water Drinking Needs

SmartKettle Byte can boil around 8.5 cups of water (1 cup of water 200mL) at a time, which is enough for one person to drink for a whole day.

300mL Thermo-flask

Hot or Cold, Perfect Every Sip

Whether you're looking to keep your hot beverages steaming or your cold drinks chilled, the mini thermo-flask ensures consistent temperature for hours. Ideal for busy mornings or tranquil evenings, make every sip perfect, every time.
