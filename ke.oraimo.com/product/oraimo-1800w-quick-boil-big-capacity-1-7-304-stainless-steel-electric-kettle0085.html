Product Parameters:

Product Features:

 

1800W Quick Boil

Drink Boiled Water Quickly

The SmartKettle View is capable of boiling at 1800 watts, which can boil water in a few seconds, thereby shortening your waiting time.

 

1.7L Big Capacity

Meet the Whole Day's Water Drinking Needs

SmartKettle View can boil around 8.5 cups of water (1 cup of water ≈ 200mL) at a time, which is enough for one person to drink for a whole day.

 

Food-grade 304 Stainless Steel

Drink Safe, Odor-free Hot Water

The interior of SmartKettle View is made of food grade 304 stainless steel, which is BPA-Free, odor-free, heat-resistant and rust-resistant. So the water is safer and it is easier to clean.

 

Clear Water Level Gauge

Easy And Timely Monitor of Water Volume

The level gauge allows easy and timely monitoring of water volume, which not only allows timely addition of water, but also avoids heating more water than you need and the danger of too much water during boiling.

 

Auto Shut-off

Boil Water at Any Time Safely

When water boils, the SmartKettle View will automatically shut off to prevent it from being burned dry and from triggering a fire.

 

Push-Button Lid

Safer to Use

Its push-button lid design ensures a gentle release of steam and eliminates splashing, and prevents you from being scalded.

 

300mL Thermo-flask   

Hot or Cold, Perfect Every Sip

Whether you're looking to keep your hot beverages steaming or your cold drinks chilled, the mini thermo-flask ensures consistent temperature for hours. Ideal for busy mornings or tranquil evenings, make every sip perfect, every time.
