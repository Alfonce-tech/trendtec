Product Features:


7-Color Projection Mood Light

The 7-Color Projection Mood Light features both fixed and cycling modes, offering an enchanting Aurora effect that elevates your space and creates a calming atmosphere for restful sleep.


8 Sound Modes 

With 8 sound modes to choose from, tailor your environment to your needs, whether meditating, sleeping, or working. These soothing sounds help relieve stress and improve sleep for all ages.


2 Mist Modes

Choose between continuous and intermittent mist modes to customize the fragrance and humidity to your preference. 


Silent Operation

Advanced noise-reduction technology keeps the diffuser below 30 dB, creating a gentle white noise to enhance sleep and support uninterrupted relaxation.


120mL Capacity

The 120mL capacity ensures extended use without frequent refills, providing consistent aroma diffusion and humidification,while the easy-fill design ensures quick and simple maintenance.
