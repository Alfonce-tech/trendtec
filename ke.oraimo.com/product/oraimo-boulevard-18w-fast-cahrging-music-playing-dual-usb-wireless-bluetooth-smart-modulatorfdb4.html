The Oraimo Boulevard 18W Car Charger (Model OCC-32D) is a versatile in-car accessory that combines rapid charging with multimedia functionality. It features dual USB-A outputs: one delivering 5V/0.5A and the other offering multiple voltage options up to 12V/1.5A, supporting 18W fast charging for efficient device power-ups. Beyond charging, it supports Bluetooth connectivity and functions as an FM transmitter, allowing wireless audio streaming through your car's sound system. Priced around KSh 1,400, the Oraimo Boulevard offers a comprehensive solution for charging and entertainment on the go.
